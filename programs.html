Our Programs

Explore our hands-on AI and coding programs designed for kids ages 6-12. Something for every skill level.

Summer Camps

AI Explorers Camp (Ages 6-8)

Beginner 1 Week Summer

An introductory camp where young learners discover what AI is, how it works, and how it shows up in everyday life. Activities include storytelling with AI, simple sorting games, and building with blocks and robots.

Sign Up

Code Builders Camp (Ages 9-12)

Intermediate 1 Week Summer

Kids dive deeper into coding with block-based and text-based programming. By the end of the week, every camper will have built their own mini app or game to take home and show off.

After-School Programs

Tech Stars (Ages 6-9)

Beginner Weekly Fall & Spring

A fun weekly after-school program where kids learn the basics of computers and coding through games, puzzles, and guided projects. Sessions run 60 minutes, once per week.

AI Lab (Ages 10-12)

Intermediate Weekly Fall & Spring

Students explore real AI concepts like machine learning, image recognition, and chatbots using kid-friendly tools. Projects are hands-on and build on each other across the semester.
